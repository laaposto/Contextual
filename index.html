Context Aggregation and Analysis

This is a demo platform aimed to facilitate the verification of the content derived exclusively from the YouTube and Facebook platforms. The demo focuses more on the video context rather than its content. To test the service, simply copy and paste a YouTube or Facebook* URL into the box, then click "Verify"

*Right click on the Facebook video and copy the video URL
